## 2024 Report Analysis

    *   *Knowledge Based:* 10
    *   *Scaling:* 2

*   **Avoided Losses:** $14.13 Million USD
*   **Actual Losses:** $39.28 Million USD

### Highlights
*   Implementation of **Liquid Carry Over models** and **multiparameter trend models** across the fleet.
*   Significant savings ($14M+) achieved through 46 confirmed successful cases.

## 2022 Report Analysis
**Source:** 2022 Annual Report (Jan-Dec)

### Overview
The 2022 report presents the value generated by the Shape/MODEC partnership, emphasizing potential avoided losses and the deployment of machine learning models for equipment monitoring.

### Key Metrics
*   **Total Successful Cases:** 19 (Listed in summary table)
*   **Potential Avoided Losses:** ~$10.35 Million USD ($10,346,540 listed in table)
*   **Total Potential Losses:** $48.13 Million USD

### Breakdown of Successful Cases (Sample)
A summary of 19 cases was presented, including:
*   **MV29:** Motor NDE Bearing temperature increase ($500k potential saving)
*   **MV30:** VRU A axial displacement ($500k potential saving)
*   **MV27:** Heat exchanger scaling deposit ($1.7M potential saving)
*   **MV26:** Crude Heater Sensor Malfunctioning ($120k potential saving)

### Highlights
*   Focus on detecting abnormalities in bearings, sensors, and scaling deposits.
*   Significant potential savings identified across various vessels (MV29, MV30, MV27, MV26, etc.).
